# triggers: jade-guests, guildmaster, information-broker

He deals in secrets and in the placement of discreet knives when necessary.
He has a hidden habit of leaving a single jade coin under a patron's cup when a favor is paid.
He moves information through the murmur of kitchens and the clink of teacups.
He has a known working relationship with character_macheonbaek when silence is required.

He is not cruel by nature; he is opportunistic by training.
He has a talent for remembering faces and for matching debts to names in a ledger.
He has a flaw: he underprices loyalty when coin is easier to spend.
He has a virtue: he values information because it can save lives if used well.
He owns a jade hairpin that he sometimes uses as a shiv when negotiations sour.
