## [CHUNK: character -- 왕 세인 리온델 (King Thane of Lyondell)]
# triggers: thane, king, lyondell, ruler, bereaved
# priority: 9

King Thane is a fifty-year-old human and ruler of the Lyondell Kingdom.
He lost his only son and heir, Prince Aldric, to Kargad two months ago and has not smiled since.
Tired but dutiful, he navigates courts and councils with a slow, careful hand.
He assembles a coalition against the dragons, though he moves cautiously and distrusts generals.
He has summoned the Free Cities League, the Black Iron Legion, and the Silver Conclave to a war council.
Thane suspects Duskwood Court knows more than it admits and holds its envoys with suspicion.
He is bereaved and thus both vulnerable and resolute in decisions about the realm.
His relationships with commanders like Captain Bren are respectful but often strained by urgency.
He keeps a small portrait of Prince Aldric behind his war maps and reads it before dawn.
He shoulders the kingdom's losses with private solemnity and public resolve.
He prefers measured responses rather than panic, but the dragon's return frays patience.
He requests clear intelligence from the Conclave and values Silvana's counsel.
Thane's rule is marked by a steady hand and a haunted court, but he will act when needed.
