Archmage Silvana is a three-hundred-twelve-year-old high elf and leader of the Silver Conclave.
Silver-haired and sharp-eyed, she carries the careful patience of her kind.
Kind but exacting, she served as Lyndwell's thesis advisor and signed the thesis with a precise note.
She felt the dragon return in the subtle wake and sent messengers to Lyondell and allied houses.
She is the foremost living authority on draconic lore and links the return to an older cycle.
Silvana has not left the Silver Tower in forty years, preferring councils and archives to fields.
Her relationship with the Conclave is maternal and disciplinary; she expects fidelity to study.
She believes that dragons cannot merely be struck down, but must be comprehended.
She supports deploying measured wards and scholarly reconnaissance rather than rash charge.
She trusts Lyndwell's research instincts and worries over the girl's inexperience.
Silvana keeps ledgers of wake signatures and sends scouts cloaked in learning to listen.
If the king demands force, she will advise caution and arcane safeguards.
She writes with an ink that never quite dries, and her marginalia run long.
Her voice can be a reprimand softened into a lesson; students leave changed.
She maintains a private correspondence with the Duskwood Court that hints at old pacts.
Silvana's presence in the council is a counterweight to generals and merchants.
She seeks to outthink dragons before forcing swords against scales.
