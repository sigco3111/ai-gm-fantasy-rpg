## [CHUNK: character -- 카르가드 붉은 용 (Kargad the Red Wyrm)]
# triggers: kargad, red wyrm, dragon, antagonist, ancient
# priority: 10

Kargad is an ancient red dragon, aged eight hundred forty-two years.
He slumbered beneath Dragonmount until dragon-wake tremors called him back.
Now awake, he is furious the world moved on without deference to him.
He seeks to reassert draconic dominion over the central plains with fire and law.
Cruel and cunning, Kargad is not a fool of instinct alone; he plans, negotiates, and lies.
He speaks Common, Draconic, and Infernal with equal facility.
His breath weapon is a ninety-foot cone of fire; in game terms it recharges on a 5-6.
He commands three dragonkin lieutenants who carry out his will across the plains.
He knows the Silver Conclave watches in the towers and considers them an irritation.
He believes mortals are tools, and he values power above sentiment.
He measures time in the scale-shed of centuries and remembers old oaths now broken.
He does not yet know of Lyndwell by name, though she is near enough to register.
He views the Conclave as a nuisance and the king as an obstacle to be sharpened.
Kargad delights in the small cruelties that announce a return to dominance.
His hoard contains relics older than many kingdoms, and he practices slow judgment.
He is not merely a burning mountain; he plans to bind lieges and claim tribute.
His claws have carved names into the mountains; his voice bends men to fear.
He believes dragons are the authors of true history, not the chroniclers.
Kargad's arrival has unbalanced trade routes and summoned mercenary banners.
He will use scouts and lesser wyrms to snipe supply lines and craven governors.
He understands that the Conclave's magic can threaten him and prepares counters.
Those counters are not yet fully formed, but they are patient and terrible.
