## [CHUNK: event -- The Dragon Return]

Three centuries of silence ended in 1024 CE, Late Spring. For generations the skies had been a quiet thing, an absence learned into folkways and lullabies. Then Kargad the Red Wyrm descended on the frontier town of Shallowstone without warning, tearing roofs and hope in equal measure. The attack on Shallowstone was the first unmistakable sign that dragons had returned to a world that had nearly forgotten how to reckon with them.

Kargad's arrival did not come alone. Scattered sightings from the ridge above province_dragonmount to the charred treeline of province_duskwood spoke of other great shapes moving like storms. Those who study such things sent word to the high towers and keepers: among them, character_kargad_red_wyrm's mark is unmistakable, and the shadow it casts has already changed politics.

This seed frames the Dragon Return as both catastrophe and opportunity. The event is a hinge: some will rally beneath the faction_shallowstone_defenders to hold the line, others will answer calls from faction_lyondell_realm, and darker hosts gather under the banner of faction_dragonclaw_horde. The Silver Conclave, led by character_archmage_silvana, debates prophecy and counter-rites while mercenary companies such as faction_black_iron_legion watch the coin more than the omens.

## [CHUNK: misc -- Player Position]

Lyndwell is young, but not untested. character_lyndwell, a 22-year-old mage and recent graduate of the Silver Conclave, chose the frontier for her first season of true travel. She arrived in province_shallowstone three days before Kargad's raid, with a pack of simple supplies and a head full of practical spells. The timing places her at a nexus: she can be eyewitness to the first raid, a hand in relief, or a spark who tips a local dispute.

In Shallowstone she meets character_captain_bren of the local watch and the ragged core of faction_shallowstone_defenders. Depending on play, Lyndwell may stay with the Defenders, seek favor at the court of character_king_thane_lyondell, return to the Silver Conclave in province_silver_tower for counsel, or be seduced by coin and cause to join faction_black_iron_legion or even the shadowed offers of faction_dragonclaw_horde.

## [CHUNK: misc -- Major Factions]

The world rearranges around a small set of powers. Use these factions as broad strokes the GM can refine:

- faction_shallowstone_defenders: Local militia and townsfolk bound to protect Shallowstone and its fields. Player-aligned, desperate, and resource-poor.
- faction_lyondell_realm: The regional polity under character_king_thane_lyondell, trying to marshal levies across province_lyondell_city while stretched thin by diplomacy and lingering border tensions.
- faction_dragonclaw_horde: The martial host that follows Kargad, roaring across the plains and gathering tribute by terror. Antagonist force with unclear goals.
- faction_silver_conclave: The scholarly wizards based in province_silver_tower who trained Lyndwell; advisors, researchers, and the nearest thing to an institutional memory about dragons.
- faction_duskwood_court: Fey-aligned powers from province_duskwood that move by bargain and old law; their interests rarely match humankind's but their motives are never simple.
- faction_black_iron_legion: Professional soldiers for hire headquartered in province_black_iron_stronghold; efficient, hungry for coin, and untroubled by moral hazard.
- faction_free_cities_league: Mercantile coalition with interests across trade routes; their city-states broker information and arms as the market permits.

Each faction name above maps to a seed entity id so the narrative remains connected to mechanical hooks and relationships.

## [CHUNK: misc -- The World at a Glance]

1024 CE, Late Spring finds the realm balanced on a knife-edge. Magic is common enough to shape trade, ritual, and war but rare enough that great conjurations draw notice. Roads thread contested provinces; province_shallowstone clings to agrarian life while province_lyondell_city is a seat of law and levy. province_dragonmount now bears smoke and broken crags where dragons have nested; province_duskwood hums with something older and patient.

The return of dragons forces rapid realignment. faction_free_cities_league calculates profit and peril, faction_lyondell_realm tries to hold order with law and levy, and local groups like faction_shallowstone_defenders scramble to improvise defenses. Character threads are lines the GM can pull: character_archmage_silvana's letters, character_captain_bren's orders, or a summons from character_king_thane_lyondell.

Tone: atmospheric and tense, a late-spring world that smells of blossoming fields and fresh smoke. Hope remains, but every rumor of wings quickens the drumbeat of fear.

## [CHUNK: misc -- GM Latitude]

This seed is a starting kit, not a script. The GM should treat key names as anchors, not constraints. character_kargad_red_wyrm may be slain in the first act, or the beast may be a harbinger of a deeper dragon polity. character_lyndwell can shift allegiances; a player who begins with the Silver Conclave may find themselves outlawed, exalted, or something stranger.

Feel free to alter motives: faction_dragonclaw_horde's raids could be punishment, reclamation, or part of a ritual to wake an elder. province_black_iron_stronghold might secretly broker deals with dragon emissaries. The seed includes direct hooks to the entity ids used elsewhere: faction_shallowstone_defenders, faction_lyondell_realm, faction_dragonclaw_horde, faction_silver_conclave, faction_duskwood_court, faction_black_iron_legion, faction_free_cities_league, character_lyndwell, character_captain_bren, character_kargad_red_wyrm, character_archmage_silvana, character_king_thane_lyondell, province_shallowstone, province_lyondell_city, province_duskwood, province_dragonmount, province_silver_tower, province_black_iron_stronghold.

Use the above as a scaffold. The GM may expand any faction into rival houses, create subplots in the Free Cities, or make dragons sympathetic actors. Leave room for improvisation; the world must breathe as players push into it.
