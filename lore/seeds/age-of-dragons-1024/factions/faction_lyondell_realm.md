## [CHUNK: faction -- 리온델 왕국 (Lyondell Kingdom)]
# triggers: lyondell, kingdom, thane, human

The Lyondell Kingdom is the largest human realm in the central plains, ruled from the walled capital of Lyondell City by King Thane. Once the dominant power across the region, Lyondell now finds its resources stretched thin by constant dragon incursions and the grief of losing the crown prince in a border skirmish.

Lyondell maintains a heavy infantry tradition supported by a small but disciplined cavalry. The kingdom seeks to hold the plains together, keeping supply lines open and rallying castellans in the surrounding provinces while hunting for ways to stem the dragon threat.
