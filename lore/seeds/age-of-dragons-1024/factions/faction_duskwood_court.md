## [CHUNK: faction -- 황혼숲 궁정 (Duskwood Court)]
# triggers: duskwood, fey, court, seelie, unseelie

The Duskwood Court rules the ancient forest of Duskwood, where Seelie and Unseelie courts maintain a fragile, ritualized balance under twin monarchs of summer and winter. The fey are isolationist and inscrutable; their priorities follow seasons and old pacts rather than human politics. Their territory is rich in old magic and fiercely protected.

Though they avoid formal alliances with human realms, the Duskwood Court has its own agenda regarding the dragon return. Mortals who trespass in their groves find bargains and riddles, and sometimes help, but never without a price.
