## [CHUNK: faction -- 검은철 군단 (Black Iron Legion)]
# triggers: black iron, legion, mercenary, sellsword

The Black Iron Legion is a feared mercenary company of roughly five thousand heavy infantry and engineers, based in the Black Iron Stronghold. They answer to contracts and coin, and their captains prize discipline and siegecraft above politics. Their ruler_id is represented by the Legion's commander, Marshal Varos, whose loyalty is to the purse.

Known for professionalism and a willingness to switch sides when paid enough, the Legion can alter the balance of local conflicts. Their engineers also maintain siege engines and fortifications, making them a strategic asset for whoever secures their contract.
