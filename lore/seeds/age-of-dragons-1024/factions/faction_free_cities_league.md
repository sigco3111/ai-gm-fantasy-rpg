## [CHUNK: faction -- 자유도시 연맹 (Free Cities League)]
# triggers: free cities, league, merchant, trade

The Free Cities League is a coalition of five merchant republics along the southern coast, their wealth built on trade and maritime routes. Officially neutral, the League's agents and merchant-princes focus on keeping commerce flowing; their home provinces prosper from ports and caravans. They will fund whichever side best protects trade and their coffers.

Pragmatic and adept at diplomacy, the League prefers to avoid open war. Their leader, Consul Maren, directs covert aid and convoy protection rather than declaring banners, seeking to preserve prosperity above ideology.
