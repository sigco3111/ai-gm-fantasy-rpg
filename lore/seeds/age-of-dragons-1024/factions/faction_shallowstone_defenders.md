## [CHUNK: faction -- 샬로우스톤 수호자 (Shallowstone Defenders)]
# triggers: militia, defenders, shallowstone, player, desperate

Shallowstone's local militia is a ragged but determined band of farmers and craftsmen who took up arms when Kargad's raid struck their province. They are led by Captain Bren, a former watchman who learned leadership in blood and smoke. The militia forms the player's home faction and has been recently devastated; many fields lie burned and families displaced.

Outmatched by professional armies and the dragonclaw's ferocity, the Shallowstone Defenders fight with grit rather than glory. Their goal is simple: protect what remains of their homes and buy time for reinforcements, even if victory seems unlikely.
