## [CHUNK: faction -- 은빛 결사 (Silver Conclave)]
# triggers: conclave, silver, mage, wizard, arcane

The Silver Conclave is an order of mages headquartered at the Silver Tower, led by Archmage Silvana, an ancient elf known for her measured counsel. Scholarly and neutral in many politics, the Conclave studies the signs of the dragon return and records arcane histories. Their halls accept talented young mages; Lyndwell is a recent graduate finding his first assignments.

While not openly martial, the Conclave's spells and wards can turn the tide of smaller engagements. They pursue knowledge first, but their research into draconic magics has made them a critical actor in the unfolding crisis.
