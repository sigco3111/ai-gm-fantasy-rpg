## [CHUNK: faction -- 용발톱 무리 (Dragonclaw Horde)]
# triggers: dragonclaw, kargad, horde, antagonist, dragon

The Dragonclaw Horde is the war-host assembled by Kargad the Red Wyrm, a brutal coalition of orcs, gnolls, kobolds, and dragon-kin cultists. Fire-aspected and expansionist, they sweep through border provinces leaving scorched earth and terror in their wake. Their warbands operate with terrifying coordination under draconic banners.

Their immediate objective is to secure territory in the central plains before the other great dragons awaken and stake their claims. The horde's tactics favor overwhelming force and terror, making them the primary antagonist in the region.
